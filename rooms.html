Rooms

Shravanam Greens offers five traditionally styled suites where you can savor the serene beauty of dawns, spectacular sunsets, the shifting colors of the sky until twilight, moonlit nights, and romantic drizzles - a beauty that defies words. All suites have a living area, bedroom, attached bathroom and balcony. There are four Type-1 suites and one Type-2 suite.

Each suite has been meticulously designed with traditional terracotta flooring in the living area and wooden flooring in the bedroom. They feature cane and wooden furniture, while all fixtures such as windows and doors have been carefully sourced and maintained from old traditional Nalukettu houses. The bedding and towels provided to the guests have been woven by the skilled artisans of Chendamangalam, the handloom village of Kerala.
