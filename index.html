Welcome to Shravanam Greens Homestay!

Step into the enchanting realm of Shravanam Greens – The Waterfront Villa, just 22km from Cochin International Airport. Nestled within a lush coconut grove, this villa exudes the charm of traditional Kerala Nalukettu architecture. The symphony of rustling leaves and birdsong creates an immersive sense of calm, while the expansive backwaters provide a mesmerizing backdrop.

Experience the tranquility of gliding on the villa's pond in a country boat, connecting with nature in its purest form. The garden showcases a vibrant array of native trees and plants, celebrating Kerala's rich heritage. Indulge in the allure of traditional Kerala cuisine and relish the pure air and cool breeze that define the atmosphere of Shravanam Greens – a timeless haven of reprieve where nature and culture converge in perfect harmony.
